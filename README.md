### Who I Am

I'm a **Senior Mobile & Backend Engineer** with 4+ years of experience shipping production systems that real users depend on daily. I don't just build features — I architect, deploy, and operate them. Right now that means managing **2 production servers running ~30 live projects** while continuing to push what's possible on mobile.

My work sits at the intersection of **real-time systems**, **clean architecture**, and **applied AI**. I integrate LLMs, voice agents, and intelligent pipelines directly into mobile products — not as experiments, but as core product features.

Currently engineering a **voice-assisted mobile agent** — a fully conversational interface powered by LiveKit, FastAPI, Flutter, and Native Kotlin that responds, reasons, and acts in real time.
